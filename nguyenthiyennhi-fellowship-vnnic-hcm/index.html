06IPv6 × Artificial Intelligence

AI scales on networks before it scales on GPUs.

Every AI model that talks to the world does it through an IP address. A single hyperscale training run can span 25,000+ globally routable nodes — IPv6 is the only viable address plane.

Networks first, then weights

  • End-to-end addressing for distributed training clusters across data centers
  • Globally routable addresses for edge AI and on-device inference fleets
  • Removal of NAT bottlenecks for low-latency federated learning
  • IPv6 + segment routing for AI-aware traffic engineering

09IPv6 × 5G Future

5G is the radio. IPv6 is the road it drives on.

5G and IPv6 are infrastructure twins. Viettel, VNPT, and MobiFone’s rollout is the natural inflection point to standardize on IPv6-only access in Vietnam.

Why 5G was designed for IPv6

  • Massive-connectivity profile (mMTC) targets 1M devices/km²
  • URLLC requires direct end-to-end paths — incompatible with deep NAT
  • 5G core (SBA) uses IPv6-native service mesh over HTTP/2
  • Network slicing leverages IPv6 flow labels for QoS

10IPv6 × Cybersecurity

IPv6 doesn’t make networks secure. It makes secure networks possible.

Honest take: the protocol removes architectural compromise — NAT, scarcity, bolt-on encryption — so modern security models can be built the way they were meant to be.

Native capabilities
  • IPSec native, not bolt-on
  • Cryptographically Generated Addresses (CGA) for node identity
  • End-to-end addressability enables zero-trust without NAT obfuscation
  • Cleaner header reduces ambiguity for inspection engines
Deployment requirements
  • Misconfiguration can expand attack surface
  • Requires workforce maturity — training is non-negotiable
  • Monitoring & governance must evolve alongside the protocol
“A modern security posture needs a modern protocol to stand on.”

12Strategic Solutions

A national transition is not a single lever. It is five, pulled together.

Government policy creates demand. Enterprises and ISPs deliver deployment. Education and research produce the people. Public awareness creates the cultural mandate.

01

Workforce & Education

IPv6 in university CS curricula, national workshops, lab infrastructure, student fellowships.

02

Enterprise Enablement

Readiness toolkits for SMEs, tax incentives, IPv6-native cloud guidance, ISP partnership programs.

03

IPv6-First Policy

Public services IPv6-only by default, procurement requirements, national monitoring dashboard.

04

Public Awareness

Media campaigns, popularized SME content, integration into digital literacy programs.

05

Research & Innovation

University labs in IPv6 × AI × IoT security, open datasets, international collaboration.

“Policy moves the country. Education moves the generation. Research moves the future.”
