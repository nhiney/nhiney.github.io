01

Smart Medical Booking App

Mobile AppFeb 2026 – Present· Team Lead & Core Developer
View Case Study

The Problem

While exploring the medical booking problem, I noticed many existing systems don't handle real-time scheduling conflicts well — leading to double-bookings and overlapping appointments. With so many hospitals and specialties to choose from, users often struggle to find the right fit. Elderly users face additional barriers due to complex interfaces with limited accessibility support.

What I Did

  • Researched user needs and designed wireframes in Figma to optimize the booking experience
  • Built a flow for users to search and compare hospitals by specialty, making selection clearer and more intuitive
  • Proposed and developed a voice-assisted feature to improve accessibility for elderly users
  • Designed a scheduling conflict check using Firebase Transactions to reduce double-bookings
  • Coordinated team tasks, sprint planning, and real-device testing

Results

  • Reduced double-booking incidents during appointment scheduling
  • Clearer, more intuitive hospital and specialty selection for users
  • More accessible experience for elderly users
  • 100+ manual test scenarios built to validate app stability
